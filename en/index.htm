Division Gros is a subsidiary of the KLARRION Consortium

Founded in 1991, more than 30 years of experience, Division Gros is an innovative trading and service company operating with a multinational network to deliver maximum value to all our customers.

Division Gros is equipped with a high level of expertise that allows us to offer our customers a wide range of steel, agricultural, agri-food, scientific, laboratory, research and development, materials and equipment, as well as equipment of machinery.
